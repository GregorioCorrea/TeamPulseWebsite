TeamPulse Support Center

Getting Started

Welcome to TeamPulse! Here's how to get started:

  1. Install TeamPulse from Microsoft AppSource
  2. Add the app to your Microsoft Teams
  3. Type "ayuda" to see available commands
  4. Create your first survey by typing "Quiero crear una encuesta"

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a survey?

Simply type in the Teams chat: "Quiero crear una encuesta sobre [topic]" and TeamPulse will guide you through the process.

Are responses really anonymous?

Yes! We use cryptographic hashing to ensure responses cannot be traced back to individuals. Not even administrators can identify respondents.

What's included in the free plan?

Free plan includes: 50 responses per survey, 1 survey per week, basic analytics, and all core features.

How do I upgrade my plan?

Go to Microsoft AppSource, find TeamPulse, and click "Get it now" to manage your subscription.

Can I export survey results?

Yes! Professional and Enterprise plans include Excel export functionality. Use the command "exportar [survey-id]".

How do I use templates?

Type "ver_templates" to see available templates, then "usar_template [template-id]" to create a survey from a template.

Available Commands

  • ayuda - Show help menu
  • listar - List all surveys
  • responder [id] - Respond to a survey
  • resultados [id] - View survey results
  • ver_templates - View survey templates
  • usar_template [id] - Use a template

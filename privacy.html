TeamPulse Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 10, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you subscribe through Microsoft AppSource, we receive your organization name, email, and Microsoft tenant ID.

Survey Data: We store survey questions, response options, and anonymized participant responses. Individual responses are anonymized using cryptographic hashing.

Usage Data: We collect usage metrics such as number of surveys created, response rates, and feature usage to improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the TeamPulse service
  • To process your subscription and payments through Microsoft
  • To generate anonymous analytics and insights from survey data
  • To send service-related communications
  • To improve our products and develop new features

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored in Microsoft Azure data centers with enterprise-grade security:

  • Data encrypted at rest using Azure Storage Service Encryption
  • Data encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2+
  • Access controlled through Azure Active Directory
  • Regular security audits and compliance monitoring

4. Data Retention

Survey data is retained for the duration of your subscription plus 90 days. After subscription cancellation, you may request immediate deletion of your data.

5. GDPR Compliance

We comply with GDPR requirements including:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to delete your data
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@incumate.io

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Microsoft Azure: Cloud infrastructure and data storage
  • Microsoft Teams: Application platform
  • Azure OpenAI: Survey creation assistance (no survey responses are shared)

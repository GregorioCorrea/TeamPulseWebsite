Product Overview

TeamPulse is an AI-powered survey and feedback bot for Microsoft Teams that enables organizations to:

  • Create professional surveys instantly using Azure OpenAI assistance
  • Collect truly anonymous responses with cryptographic hashing
  • Generate real-time insights and analytics
  • Use pre-built templates for HR, customer feedback, training, and 360¬∞ reviews
  • Maintain enterprise-grade security with Azure cloud storage
  • Integrate seamlessly with existing Teams workflows
